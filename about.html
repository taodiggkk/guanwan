ABOUT MORE OPTICS


We are a professional reticle manufacturer. With over 20 years of experience in the optical reticle technology field, we process all kinds of reticles, gratings, coders, modulators, resolution plates, raster plates and other high-class photoetching products. Our products are the key components of scope sights, telescopes, theodolites, total stations, sight levels, surgical microscopes, refractometers, planetariums, etc.

As a professional reticle manufacturer, we have a solid theoretical foundation. Our chief engineer Huaidao Liu is the main drafter of "Chinese National Standards for Optical Reticle Components". We believe strict quality control guarantees the precision and reliability of our products, and our strict quality inspection process covers Material, Substrate, Pattern, Coating, etc. "A product at its best means putting precision first" is our motto. We have a refined and thorough quality control system, and our factory has passed the international ISO9001 quality certification system since 2004, and it has been operating consistently and effectively for years.

We have a wide gamut of technical equipment used in all stages of our manufacturing process—from design, production, to inspection. We can make patterns in different substrates—from optical glass and optical quartz to far UV and IR materials. Our mature processing techniques combine traditional mechanical etching technology, conventional photographic copy technology, as well as precise photoetching technology. All of our products are fully customizable, and we welcome you to contact us for details.
