About More Optics

We are a professional reticle manufacturer. With over 20 years of experience in the Optical Reticles technology field, we process all kinds of reticles, gratings, coders, modulators, resolution plates, raster plates and other high-class photoetching products. Our products are the key components of scope sights, telescopes, theodolites, total stations, sight levels, surgical microscopes, refractometers, planetariums, etc.
